An Invitation to The City

The world of Project Moon is dominated by a single, sprawling megalopolis known only as The City. It is a place of stark contrasts, where unimaginable technology born from "Singularities" coexists with soul-crushing dystopia. The City is ruled by 26 hyper-corporate entities known as "The Wings," each with its own laws, ethics, and monstrous power.

From the creature-containment halls of Lobotomy Corporation, to the fateful duels in the Library of Ruina, and the desperate pilgrimage of the Sinners in Limbus Company, every story is a struggle for survival, identity, and a sliver of humanity in a world that has forgotten its meaning. We are here to document it all.
